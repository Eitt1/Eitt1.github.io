Privacy Policy

                
            ⚠️ NOTICE: This is a student project for educational purposes only.             Do not upload sensitive personal data or copyrighted materials.         
        

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

        

Welcome to Media Cloud ("we," "our," or "us"). We represent a student group project dedicated to demonstrating Cloud Computing and Web Development skills. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

        

1. Information We Collect

        

We collect the following types of information when you use our application:

        
                
  • Account Information: When you log in via Facebook, we collect your public profile name and profile picture to display your user identity.
    •             
  • User Content: Any media files (MP3, MP4) you explicitly upload to our server, along with their titles and descriptions.
    •             
  • Cookies: We use session cookies to keep you logged in while you navigate our site.
    •         
        

2. How We Use Your Information

        
                
  • To provide and maintain the Media Cloud service.
    •             
  • To identify you as the uploader of specific files.
    •             
  • To demonstrate database management and file storage capabilities for our course project.
    •         
        

3. Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

        

In accordance with Facebook Platform rules, we provide a way for users to request data deletion. Since this is a demo application, we do not permanently retain user data beyond the evaluation period.

        

If you wish to remove your data immediately, please:

        
                
  1. Go to your Facebook Account "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings".
    2.             
  2. Look for "Apps and Websites" and verify the "Media Cloud" app.
    3.             
  3. Remove the app.
    4.             
  4. Alternatively, you can manually delete your uploaded files using the "Delete" button on our list page.
    5.         
        

4. Data Storage and Security

        

Your data is stored in a MongoDB Atlas cluster. While we implement basic security measures (authentication), please be aware that this is an experimental application. We strictly advise against uploading confidential, private, or sensitive documents.

        

5. Contact Us

        

If you have any questions about this project, please contact the project instructor or the team representative.
